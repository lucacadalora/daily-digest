China's Steel Sector Seized by Talk of 'Supply Reform 2.0'

By Luca Cada Lora • March 2, 2025

China's billion-ton steel industry is edging toward its biggest shake-up in a decade, with speculation growing that Beijing will order plant closures in response to a construction slowdown at home and a wave of protectionism overseas.

The world's biggest steel market has been abuzz this week with speculation that Beijing will mandate capacity cuts of 50 million tons – possibly as early as a key political gathering in the capital next week. No plans have been announced, but the widespread speculation reflect broad recognition that the struggling steel industry needs a fresh overhaul.
